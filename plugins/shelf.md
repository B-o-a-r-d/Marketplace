**Shelf** turns a board into a document shelf — a Trilium-like living next to
your kanban boards, inheriting per-board members and roles, pinning and the
board switcher.

- **Explorer tree** — folders, notes and files: inline create/rename, drag &
  drop, context menus, live updates for every viewer, soft trash with a 30-day
  purge.
- **Markdown notes** — TipTap editor with autosave, slash commands and tables;
  **realtime co-editing with named cursors** (CRDT synced over the host's
  websocket — no extra server); automatic revisions with a diff/restore panel.
- **Files & previews** — multi-file dropzone, image lightbox, inline PDF
  viewer, video/audio players, authorized downloads.
- **Quota** — instance-wide default (admin setting) with a per-board override;
  notes, revisions and files all count.
- **Imports/exports** — `.md`/`.txt` become editable notes, `.zip` archives
  unpack as a tree, notes export as raw markdown. No external binaries.
- **Search & MCP** — find nodes by name or note content; list/read/write/
  create/move from an AI agent through the MCP server.

Once active, the dashboard's board creation form offers **Board / Shelf**.
